Birds of Missouri

The American Robin is common in North American backyards and lawns. It is brown with an orange breast and a yellow beak. However, its eggs are sky-blue. You may be able to find its eggshells on the ground in spring. It eats worms and hunts for them on lawns. It sings with cheery chirps.

Common backyard birds of Missouri include

  • American Robin
  • Common Grackle
  • Mourning Dove
